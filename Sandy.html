Say Hi! to Sandy

Don't miss the chance to say hi to Sandy. Sandy is our sealion pal who loves to meet new people and to entertain them. He does a cute little dance to our visitors to express his excitement. He also does some ring jump to impress the visitors. He spents his rest of the time with his favourite toy pal "Ball". Our pal Sandy also likes get fed by the visitors. We also facilitate our visitors with Sandy's favourite food to feed him. Sandy more loves to get fed by children. Give your children a chance to feed our pal Sandy.

About us

Welcome to the official webpage of Dehiwala Zoo. Being one of the oldest zoos in Asia, there are varieties of birds, mammals, reptiles, insects, and aquatic animals. Not just with fauna, the place has a remarkable amount of flora too. A variety of programmes to entertain the visitors and to attract tourists are also orchestrated.
