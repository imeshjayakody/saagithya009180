Our Rules

In our zoo, rules are made for the well being of our guests and the animals. We kindly request all the visitors to follow the rules we made and the signs we posted to avoid inconveniences. Our zoo staffs will kindly warn you in situations of violating the rules. We also inspect any item brought onto Zoo property.

  1. Please be kind to the animals and we kindly request you to give the respect they deserve.
  2. Please avoid causing any harm to any plants at the premises.
  3. Do not try to feed animals or take pictures with or near them.
  4. Do not throw litter everywhere. Please use the respective bin for respective waste.
  5. Keep your children under your supervison.
  6. No pets.
  7. Smoking in the premises is strictly prohibited. You'll be fined under violation.
  8. Your belongings, your responsibility.
  9. Please avoid feeding the animals since it may be destructive for them.
  10. We kindly expect you all to behave appropriate to the situation.
  11. Visitors are allowed to do following activities on their own risk, within the given space;
    • Cycling
    • Skateboarding
    • Inline Skating
    • Hoverboard Rides
    • Scooter Rides

About us

Welcome to the official webpage of Dehiwala Zoo. Being one of the oldest zoos in Asia, there are varieties of birds, mammals, reptiles, insects, and aquatic animals. Not just with fauna, the place has a remarkable amount of flora too. A variety of programmes to entertain the visitors and to attract tourists are also orchestrated.
